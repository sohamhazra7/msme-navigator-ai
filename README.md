# MSME Navigator AI

A Multi-Agent Business Consulting System for Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)

## Overview

MSME Navigator AI is a multi-agent business consulting platform designed to help small and medium enterprises identify business challenges and receive actionable recommendations.

The system combines specialized AI agents that analyze financial health, credit readiness, growth opportunities, and government support programs before generating a consolidated business recommendation report.

## Problem Statement

Many MSMEs struggle with:

- Limited access to professional consulting services
- Working capital and cash flow issues
- Difficulty obtaining business financing
- Lack of awareness regarding government schemes
- Uncertainty around business growth strategies

MSME Navigator AI aims to provide accessible, structured, and affordable guidance through an AI-powered multi-agent system.

## Solution Architecture

The system consists of multiple specialized agents:

### Coordinator Agent
Receives user inputs and coordinates tasks among specialized agents.

### Financial Agent
Analyzes:
- Financial health
- Cash flow concerns
- Working capital issues
- Profitability indicators

### Credit Agent
Evaluates:
- Credit readiness
- Financing opportunities
- Credit-related risks

### Growth Agent
Provides:
- Market expansion suggestions
- Operational improvement recommendations
- Business growth opportunities

### Government Scheme Agent
Identifies:
- Relevant MSME support programs
- Subsidies and incentives
- Government-backed financing schemes

### Consultant Agent
Combines outputs from all agents and generates a final business recommendation report.

## Workflow

User Input

↓

Coordinator Agent

↓

Financial Agent | Credit Agent | Growth Agent | Government Scheme Agent

↓

Consultant Agent

↓

Business Recommendation Report
